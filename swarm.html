03 / Roles
Swarm composition

Eleven roles.

Build a swarm by mixing these. Each one specializes; together they ship.

Strategist

Reads the brief. Sets the route. Assigns who ships what.

Scout

Pulls market intelligence. Finds reference patterns and prior art.

Builder

Ships the actual artifact. Code, copy, design, automation.

Critic

Redteams everything. Surfaces the weakest link. Forces fixes.

Researcher

Deep-dives unknowns. Returns with citations and tradeoffs.

Refiner

Polishes. Tightens. Removes the seams. Last 10% before ship.

Defender

Accessibility, security, edge cases, what could break.

Negotiator

Cross-swarm comms. Resolves overlap. Trades resources.

Optimizer

Performance, token-efficiency, cost. Makes it lean.

Closer

Deploys. Documents. Hands off. Confirms shipped state.

Debugger

Drops in when something breaks mid-mission. Pure fix-mode.
